Penne alla vodka (Italian pronunciation: [ˈpenne alla ˈvɔdka]) is a pasta dish made primarily with vodka and penne pasta (or similar shapes such as rigatoni), usually accompanied with heavy cream, crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce, onions, and sometimes small meats and vegetables like sausage, pancetta or peas.
